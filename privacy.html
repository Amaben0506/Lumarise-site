Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

LumaRise is a manifestation, affirmation, and journaling app designed to support reflection, clarity, and personal growth. Your privacy matters to us.

Information We Collect

LumaRise may collect user-generated content you choose to enter into the app, such as goals, journal entries, affirmations, and text submitted to generate AI affirmations.

How We Use Information

Information you provide is used to personalize your experience, generate affirmations, save journal entries, and support app functionality.

AI Features

When you use AI-powered features, the text you submit may be sent securely to our backend service and AI provider to generate a response. We do not sell your journal entries, affirmations, or personal reflections.

Data Storage

Some app data may be stored locally on your device, including saved goals, journal entries, and affirmations.

Third-Party Services

LumaRise may use third-party services to provide app functionality, including AI generation and Apple in-app purchases.

Children’s Privacy

LumaRise is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Choices

You may clear saved app data from within the app where available. You may also contact us with privacy questions or support requests.
